🌟Science project on State of Matter.. Grade 4 Students...📚

This Science Project helps Grade 4 students understand the three states of matter — solids, liquids, and gases — through simple experiments and creative activities. Students learn the properties of each state, how matter changes from one form to another, and how these changes occur in everyday life. The project encourages observation, teamwork, scientific thinking, and hands-on learning in a fun and engaging way.

Skeleton 🩻 Project by Grade 4 Science students...

This creative Science Project helped Grade 4 students explore the human skeletal system in a fun and interactive way. Students learned about the major bones in the body, their functions, and the importance of keeping bones healthy. Through hands-on activities and model making, the project encouraged creativity, teamwork, and scientific understanding while making learning enjoyable and memorable.🌟
