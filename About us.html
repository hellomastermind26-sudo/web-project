Who We Are

Master Mind Learning Center is a dedicated educational institute focused on nurturing young learners through quality education and guided learning experiences. We provide structured classes for different grade levels, ensuring that every student receives individual attention and support.

Our team of qualified and passionate teachers is committed to developing not only academic excellence but also discipline, creativity, and confidence in every student. We believe education is the foundation for a successful and meaningful life.

Message from the Founder

We are committed to providing high-quality education that nurtures young minds and prepares students for a successful future. Our goal is to create a supportive, engaging, and inspiring learning environment for every student.

Our Mission

To protect and promote the rights of every Sri Lankan to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment. Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner. Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.

Our Vision

We dream of Sri Lankans who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. As a learner-centered public institution, we strive to shape responsible, confident, and capable individuals.

Our Story

Established in 2017, Maze Mind Learning Center was founded with a clear vision: to provide high-quality, personalised education to students seeking academic excellence. The center is led by a highly qualified educator and former principal of three prestigious international schools, bringing a wealth of experience and leadership to our institution. We specialize in the Edexcel Pearson syllabus, offering classes from primary levels through to O/L (Ordinary Level) examinations. Over the past seven years, we have expanded our offerings to include a wide range of subjects and grade levels, catering to the diverse needs of our student community. Our teaching team comprises highly qualified, experienced, and dedicated professionals, each with a deep understanding of the Edexcel Pearson curriculum. At Maze Mind Learning Center, we are committed to guiding students towards academic success in a supportive and enriching environment.

Teaching Approach & Achievements

We focus on clear teaching methods, individual attention, and continuous assessment. Our students have consistently shown excellent academic progress and achieved outstanding results.
